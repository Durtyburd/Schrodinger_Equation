About

Welcome, I am excited to present a powerful Schrödinger equation solver. Through extensive dedication and exploration, I have created a sophisticated tool that utilizes the Finite-Difference Time-Domain (FDTD) method to simulate and solve the Schrödinger equation.

This project allows me to delve into the depths of quantum mechanics, visualizing wave functions, energy levels, and quantum dynamics. With a user-friendly interface, you can input parameters and witness real-time visualizations of quantum phenomena directly on the screen.

Join me in this immersive journey as I unlock the secrets of quantum physics and contribute to the advancement of scientific knowledge. Discover the captivating world of the Schrödinger equation and explore the realm of quantum mechanics through this unique, self-developed research project.
